audio/cs/ — OPTIONAL

This folder is NOT required. The sounds that are already configured live in
audio/game/ and work without it.

Use it only if you want to swap/add sounds with your own files
(e.g. sounds from your installed CS 1.6, in cstrike/sound/). After copying,
register the paths in audio/manifest.json under the "cs" key.

Available keys: "awp", "pistol", "reload", "roundstart", "footsteps".
